### **"No Properties Found"**

**Possible causes:**

- âœ… Wrong Google account selected
- âœ… Properties not verified in GSC
- âœ… Insufficient permissions (need Owner or Full User)

**Solution:**

1. Visit [<u>Google Search Console</u>](https://search.google.com/search-console)
2. Verify you see your properties there
3. Check your permission level
4. Try connecting again with the correct account

### **"Authorization Failed"**

**Possible causes:**

- âœ… Pop-up blocker interfering
- âœ… Browser privacy settings too strict
- âœ… Cookies disabled

**Solution:**

1. Enable pop-ups for [copyrocket.ai](http://copyrocket.ai)
2. Try in incognito/private mode
3. Use a different browser
4. Clear browser cache

### **"Data Not Syncing"**

**Possible causes:**

- âœ… New property (no historical data)
- âœ… GSC API rate limits
- âœ… Token expired

**Solution:**

1. Wait 5-10 minutes and refresh
2. Disconnect and reconnect property
3. Contact support if issue persists

### **"Insufficient Permissions"**

**You need at least "Full User" access in GSC.**

**To check permissions:**

1. Open [<u>Google Search Console</u>](https://search.google.com/search-console)
2. Click **Settings** (gear icon)
3. Click **Users and Permissions**
4. Verify your permission level
