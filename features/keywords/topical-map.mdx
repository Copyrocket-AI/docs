Topical Map helps you build topical authority for your core topic. 

## Step 1: Shortlist your Core Topic 

- It can be like "Home Automation"
- Make sure you add your country and langauge in it, For example, "Home Automation in US" or write in your regional language

<Info>
  Pro Tip: Shortlist 3 and 4 core topic to get 1200+ topics in one go
</Info>

## Step 2: Generate your First Topical Map

![Clean Shot 2026 01 21 At 15 41 14@2x](/images/CleanShot2026-01-21at15.41.14@2x.png)

- Go to [**Topical Map**](https://ma.copyrocket.ai/topical-map) from left sidebar
- Paste your first core keyword 
- Click on **Generate Map** 
- Wait for few minutes to complete

## Step 3: Review and Download Your Topical Map

![Clean Shot 2026 01 21 At 15 43 29@2x](/images/CleanShot2026-01-21at15.43.29@2x.png)

- Click on CSV Button to download the file. 
- Add to task button to add topic to your personal CRM To do list
- Click on AI Write button to start writing straight away.
