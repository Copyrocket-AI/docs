Analyze domain rankings, find competitors, and discover keyword opportunities

## What it Does?

![Clean Shot 2026 01 21 At 00 26 56@2x](/images/CleanShot2026-01-21at00.26.56@2x.png)

- Find Your **competitors**.
- Find **Keywords** from Competitor's Domain.
- Find **Lost Keywords** from your or Competitor's Domain.
- Find **Historical Keywords** from your or Competitor's Domain.
- Find **Intersection Keywords** from your and Competitor's Domain.

## How to find your Competitor?

![Clean Shot 2026 01 21 At 00 30 55@2x](/images/CleanShot2026-01-21at00.30.55@2x.png)

- Navigate to **Competitive Research** tool from left sidebar. 
- Enter your domain in **Target domain** section, Select country and language filter and click on "**Analyze**".
-   Then under **Organic Competitors** list you will find your competitors domains. 

## How to Find Keywords from your Competitor's domain?

![Clean Shot 2026 01 21 At 00 33 51@2x](/images/CleanShot2026-01-21at00.33.51@2x.png)

- Once You have shortlisted your competitor's keyword then replace that in Target domain section of the tool. 
- Select desired country, language and click on Analyze. 
- Then, under keywords tab, you will find current, lost and historical keywords.

## How to Find intersection keywords

![Clean Shot 2026 01 21 At 00 36 46@2x](/images/CleanShot2026-01-21at00.36.46@2x.png)

Intersection keywords are those which you and competitor's are competing. 

- Enter your Target domain and type competitors domain.
- Select country and language filter and click on Analyze. 
-  Under Intersection tab you will find all those keywords that you and your competitor's are ranking. 

## Find Competitor's Ranking Positions

![Clean Shot 2026 01 21 At 00 39 57@2x](/images/CleanShot2026-01-21at00.39.57@2x.png)

- Under same report Select **Positions** tab and You will find all **Position Distribution**
- **Position from 4 -10, 11-20, 21-30 and so on.**
