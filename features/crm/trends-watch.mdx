## Fetch Realtime Content From News

- Type your niche or core topic you want to track online
- For example AI, SEO, Dog health, Car Dashcam
- Click on search news 
- You will find latest updated news as "Google News" Card.

## Add Feeds from Popular Sources

- Add Feeds from popular Sources in your niche
- For most cases its "{url}/feed.xml"
- If you don't know how then use the apps listed below.

## Apps to Fetch Feed URL

- [Chrome Extension](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/get-rss-feed-url/kfghpdldaipanmkhfpdcjglncmilendn?ref=copyrocket.ai): Just browse any website and get it's feed URL.
- [rssfinder.app](http://rssfinder.app) to find feeds URL from your Niche.
- [rss.app](http://rss.app) to find feeds URL from Social Media like Instagram, X, Linkedin and more.
